✦ Secure Refund & Payment Policy

Refund Policy

SkillSprings is committed to maintaining a transparent and fair refund process. Please review our refund terms carefully before purchasing any digital courses or services.

💳

Eligibility for Refunds

Refund requests are reviewed individually. Users may request refunds within the eligible refund period after course purchase.

  • Refund request must be genuine
  • Duplicate transactions are refundable
  • Technical issues may qualify for refunds
  • Requests are reviewed within 7 business days
📚

Non-Refundable Digital Products

Since SkillSprings provides instant digital access, certain purchases may become non-refundable after significant course consumption.

  • Downloaded course materials
  • Completed course modules
  • Issued certificates
  • Lifetime access activated courses
🔐

Payment Gateway Policy

All payments are processed securely using Razorpay. SkillSprings does not store any sensitive banking or card details.

  • SSL encrypted transactions
  • Secure payment processing
  • Protected Razorpay checkout
  • PCI-compliant infrastructure
⏳

Refund Timeline

Approved refunds are generally processed within 7 to 10 business days depending on the payment provider and bank processing time.
